Cooked Resume

AI-Powered Resume Review Tool

How it works

Cooked Resume is a tool that students can use to get feedback on their resumes. This tool was designed to be used by students looking to get their very first or even second internship, as that is what the resume guides are intended for. The feedback is generated by OpenAI's ChatGPT-4.1 model, however it is given appropriate and ample context before formulating it's response. Specifically, it is following resume guides designed for engineering students. The resume guides were sourced from University of Waterloo and Universty of Toronto students. To download the resume guides directly, scroll down and click "Download Resume Guides".
